About Me

My name is Kagiso Mahoko from Vaal in Gauteng. I love expressing my creativity through design and illustration. It all started at college when I was studying film and I would design posters and covers, people loved my work so they wanted me to design more things from logos to business cards. Since then I've been freelancing as a graphic designer for more than 5 years.

I enjoy working on a project from concept till it comes to life but my biggest joy is having a happy client after seeing how I translated their brief to visuals. My best friend is Photoshop and I love the quality that comes with Illustrator. When I'm not staring at a computer screen, I play with brushes and paint to express my self.
